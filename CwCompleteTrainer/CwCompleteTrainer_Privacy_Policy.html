CW Complete Trainer - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2025

We respect your privacy. CW Complete Trainer does not collect, store, or transmit personal data.

Permissions Used

  • READ_PHONE_STATE: Used only to detect incoming calls and auto-pause playback. No data is collected or shared.
  • INTERNET: Used only when fetching news articles (on user request). Downloaded content is stored locally and never transmitted.

Offline Operation

Most features work completely offline without internet.

Data Storage

  • User settings (WPM, volume, etc.) are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. No sync to cloud. No analytics.
  • Game scores and history are stored locally only.

No Third-Party Services

  • No ads
  • No tracking
  • No analytics
  • No telemetry

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Please check back occasionally for updates.
