HB9HKN is a Swiss amateur radio operator with a passion for CW (Morse code). Licensed in 1982, a long career break was followed by a return to the hobby, bringing fresh enthusiasm for CW. Approximately 95% of on-air time is spent on CW across the 10 – 40 metre bands.

CW Complete Trainer App

CW Complete Trainer

A free Android app for learning and practising Morse code — built by HB9HKN. Covers everything from individual characters (Koch method) through words, sentences, QSO simulations, and live web article conversion.

  • Koch method character training (40 characters, LCWO order)
  • Common words, HAM abbreviations, and callsign training
  • Realistic QSO, SOTA, and POTA simulations
  • Four Quick Game modes with Speed Ladder
  • QRM / QRN interference simulation
  • Text-to-Speech confirmation overlay
  • CW web browsing — convert any article to Morse audio
